Aladdin writes a word on the sand and gave a task to Abu to count the vowels in the word.As Abu is your favorite character , help Abu in completeing his task.
Letters in tha word are assumed to be in lower case.

Input:
First line contains the test case( say T)
Next T lines contains words.

Output:
For each test case print T lines ,each line contain the number of vowels in the given word.


Example:

Input:
2
aladdin
abu

Output:
3
2

Constraints:
Maximum length of word will be 5000.
